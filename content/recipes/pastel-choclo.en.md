{{< lead >}}
Chilean corn pie with a beef and chicken filling, topped with a ground corn layer baked golden.
{{< /lead >}}

# Ingredients
{{< keyword icon="user" >}} Makes several individual pies {{< /keyword >}}

### For the filling (pino)
* Onion
* Ground beef
* Chicken
* Beef broth
* Oregano
* Ají de color (Chilean paprika-style seasoning)
* Garlic

### For the corn topping (pastelera)
* Corn
* Basil (as much as fits in your hand)
* Red bell pepper
* Milk

### To assemble
* Eggs (boiled)
* Sugar

# Step by step
{{< keyword icon="clock" >}} Filling + topping ~40 min, oven ~20 min {{< /keyword >}}

### 1. Boil the chicken and eggs
Boil the chicken and the eggs separately until cooked through.

### 2. Make the filling (pino)
In a pot, combine the beef broth, oregano, the onion in small cubes, ají de color, garlic, and the ground beef. Cook until the onion turns translucent.

### 3. Add the chicken
Shred the cooked chicken and add it to the filling.

### 4. Add the egg
Chop the boiled egg and mix it into the filling.

### 5. Make the corn topping (pastelera)
Blend the corn and add the basil (as much as fits in one hand) together with the red bell pepper. Add milk and cook until the topping comes together; the flavor should shift toward the corn. About 30 min.

### 6. Assemble the pies
Assemble each pie using the filling as the base and the corn topping as the top layer. Sprinkle sugar over the topping layer.

### 7. Bake
Put it in the oven, preheated to the max, and leave it until the sugar turns golden. About 20 min.

# Notes / Tips
- The oven runs at max from the start, so watch the sugar browning visually rather than trusting the clock.
- The filling and the corn topping can be made in parallel while the chicken and eggs boil separately.
